What I do?

Otto

Wills

Otto Wills is a record producer and lyricist out of Northeastern North Carolina. Having been born and raised in Windsor, NC, Isaac became "Otto" once he attended Elizabeth City State University to study Music Technology. His unique style, versatile craft, and unruly hair are what got the name "Otto" to stick.Classically trained on the piano since the age of 7, Otto also plays the guitar and trumpet, and is known to have mastered music theory and aural skills.

DISCLAIMER: ASIDE FROM SOME PAID RECORDING SESSIONS, OTTO PRODUCES, ARRANGES, WRITES AND MASTERS ALL HIS OWN MATERIAL.

Otto Wills Presents

The Moral Supper of '16

Otto Wills debuts his first official recorded LP, "The Moral Supper of '16 on December 20, 2016. This project immediately shot up to #1 in the area. It's a hell of a start for the reserved artist, as rhythmic overflows meet what Otto describes as "Trip-Hop," the budding subgenre of Hip-Hop intertwining experimental sounds, cloudy chords, and abstract syncopations.
